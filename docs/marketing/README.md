# MakeGraph - Core Marketing Narrative

## One-Sentence Positioning

MakeGraph is a free online chart maker for Excel and CSV data.

Upload or paste your spreadsheet, generate bar charts, line charts, and pie charts in 1 minute, then export PNG or SVG instantly. No signup, no registration, completely free.

## Core Value

MakeGraph is built for one common job: turn spreadsheet data into clear charts fast.

Users should not need to learn a BI platform, configure dozens of options, or spend extra time polishing visuals just to finish a basic chart. The product focuses on the shortest path from data input to usable output.

## Core Message Pillars

- Works with Excel and CSV data
- Supports upload or direct paste workflow
- Generates bar charts, line charts, and pie charts in about 1 minute
- Exports PNG or SVG instantly
- No signup, no registration, completely free

## Short Product Description

MakeGraph helps people turn Excel and CSV data into clear charts without setup friction. Upload or paste a spreadsheet, generate a chart in about a minute, and export PNG or SVG immediately.

## Homepage Message

### Title

Free online chart maker for Excel and CSV data

### Description

Upload or paste your spreadsheet, generate bar charts, line charts, and pie charts in 1 minute, then export PNG or SVG instantly. No signup, no registration, completely free.

## Messaging Rules

- Lead with the input source: Excel, CSV, spreadsheet data
- Lead with the main action: upload or paste
- Lead with the main outcome: generate charts in 1 minute
- Always mention PNG or SVG export in short descriptions when space allows
- Keep "No signup" and "completely free" visible in homepage-style copy
- Avoid generic statements like "data visualization platform" or "business intelligence tool"

## Audience Framing

### Primary

- People who already have spreadsheet data
- People making charts for reports, slides, or quick analysis
- People who want fast output instead of advanced setup

### Secondary

- Students and teachers
- Operators and marketers
- Content teams and analysts

## Reusable Copy Blocks

### Short CTA

- Create Chart Online
- Upload Spreadsheet
- Paste Data and Start
- Export PNG or SVG

### Short Trust Line

- No signup
- Completely free
- Ready in 1 minute
- Works with Excel and CSV

## What to Avoid

- Overstating advanced analytics capabilities
- Leading with niche chart types before the spreadsheet workflow
- Describing the product as a design-heavy chart studio
- Using inconsistent promises across README, docs, and site metadata
